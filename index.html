Samyaprāś Ashram – Vrindavan

Meaning: Samyaprāś = Sacred intake in balance. A place to Feel, Deal, and Heal.

Vision: A 10-acre Tapovan-style, zero-AC, eco-sustainable ashram for dharmic healing and spiritual living in Vrindavan.

Design Philosophy: Inspired by Bhagavatam, ecology, and traditional Indian wisdom. Built with mud, bamboo, and lime — powered by solar and biogas.

Zones:

  • Spiritual Core (Temple, Bhajan Hall, Yajna Shala)
  • Residential Kutirs (150+ beds)
  • Goshala with biogas plant
  • Organic farming, water harvesting, and forest belts

Mottos: "Feel the Earth, Heal the Soul" | "Rooted in Dharma, Growing in Grace"
